Notes

  1. For both scripts, Above diacritics create separate phonemes; not variants & Below diacritics encode semantic meaning
  2. Þ & Ð are separate phonemes, differing in voicing (θ & ð)
  3. ◌̨ is the Emphasis mark in Śrątik, though it is absent from K̭ạṣḳạḍịk̬
  4. Śrątik is an Abugida (X is the Null Consonant, hence why it's absent from K̭ạṣḳạḍịk̬)
  5. K̭ạṣḳạḍịk̬ is an Alphabet
  6. Unlike most abugidas, Śrątik consonants have no inherent vowel
  7. Ǔ̥ is absent from Śrątik, as the dialect does not have the sound, but the K̭ạṣḳạḍịk̬ dialect does
  8. Numerals in both scripts are base-12 (0-11)
  9. Śrątik is blocky, horizontal & carved in stone
  10. K̭ạṣḳạḍịk̬ is curved, vertical & etched along the wood grain
  11. K̭ạṣḳạḍịk̬ has solo, initial, medial & final letter forms
